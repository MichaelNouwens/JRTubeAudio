About

. . . passion for music, design flair, attention to detail . . . important factors that contribute to the ultimate culmination of the hand crafted tube amplifiers that Jean Riëtte proudly brings to you. With a keen interest to never stop learning, and many years of experience, master craftsman Jean Nouwens takes pride in his work. From winding transformers in house to ensuring the careful soldering of each component and wire in place, no detail is too small to go unnoticed.
